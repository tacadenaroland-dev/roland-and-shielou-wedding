Our Story

From childhood classmates to lifelong partners

Our journey began in the halls of our elementary and high school. We shared the same campus all the way to college, taking different paths but always staying friends. After graduation, life led us to different places. But one day, in one of our playful conversations, we made a promise: "If we're both still single at 30, we'll end up together." We laughed then, not knowing how real it would become.

Years later, fate brought us back through a reunion. One conversation turned into many, messages turned into feelings, and soon we realized — this time, it was more than friendship. What started as a promise became a love story. And now, we're keeping that promise — forever.
